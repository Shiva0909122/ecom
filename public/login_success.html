Caramel Chocolate Cake

The foundation of the Caramel Chocolate Cake is moist and tender chocolate cake layers.
These layers are typically made with a combination of flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, milk, and other ingredients.
The result is a deeply chocolatey and flavorful cake that serves as the base for the dessert.
One of the star elements of this cake is the caramel sauce.
The caramel is made by melting sugar until it reaches a rich amber color, then combining it with butter, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. The result is a smooth and velvety caramel sauce that adds sweetness, depth, and a luxurious touch to each bite.
